How this works
Understanding the balance check
Total Debit — net money out from your accounts this month (from the numbered Excel sheets).
Exclusions — amounts in total debit that are not daily spending: e-wallet top-ups, inter-account transfers, piutang (loans out), etc.
Expected in detail = Total Debit − Exclusions. This is what should appear in your categorized transactions.
Actual in detail — sum of all debit transactions recorded in Supabase for this month.
Selisih = 0 ✓ means everything is accounted for. A positive selisih means some spending is missing from your detail records.
